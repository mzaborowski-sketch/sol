Contact the Polish Hub

Questions about the Hub, its work programme, working groups and opportunities for cooperation should be addressed to the Secretariat.

What can you contact us about?

The Secretariat welcomes questions about Hub organisation, the scope of work, the initial working groups and opportunities to contribute experience, data or a proposed topic.

This contact is not a formal application form. It is intended to start a conversation and determine whether and how an institution or expert may contribute to the work.
